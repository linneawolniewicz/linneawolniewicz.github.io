Hello!

My name is Linnea Wolniewicz, and I am passionate about using machine learning to understand the universe around us.
I am a Computer Science Ph.D. student in Dr. Peter Sadowski's Machine Learning Lab at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa and a National Science Foundation Fellow. I received my M.Sc. from UH Mānoa in Computer Science in August, 2024 and my B.A. from the University of Colorado Boulder in Astrophysics in May, 2022.
I am interested in using scientific domain knowledge to inform machine learning models, or more broadly AI for science.
In my free time I can be found playing the harp, climbing, hiking, or surfing the waves of Oʻahu.
